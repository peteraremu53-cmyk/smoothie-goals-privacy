Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

👋 Overview

Welcome to Smoothie Goals. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to being transparent about our practices.

Smoothie Goals is a simple smoothie recipe app. It does not require account creation or login, and does not collect any personal information from its users.

📦 Data We Collect

Smoothie Goals does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Health or fitness data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

💾 Data Retention

Since Smoothie Goals does not collect any personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers. The only data stored is your saved favorite recipes, which is kept locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.

We retain no user data. There is nothing stored on our end to retain or delete.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Since no personal data is collected or stored by Smoothie Goals, there is no data to delete. If you wish to clear any locally stored preferences (such as saved favorites), you can do so by uninstalling the app from your device.

If you have any concerns, you may contact us at the email below and we will respond within 30 days.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Smoothie Goals does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

👶 Children's Privacy

Smoothie Goals is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, no special measures are required.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
